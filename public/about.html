WHO WE ARE

Our Founder Sean Cosentino had an idea. Like many other people he sought to develop this idea; in attempting that path he met with over 100 development shops. In doing so one thing became highly apparent, there was no consistency in the market. Dev shops price every project differently, and have varying collaboration methodologies. Sean wanted to be very involved with his project, yet it seemed that collaboration was a premium not a necessity. With that in mind Sean learned to program through an intensive coding bootcamp, and set out to tackle the issues of transparency and collaboration in the deveopment market.
